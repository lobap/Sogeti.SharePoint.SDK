===========================================================
Instructions for importing Product Catalog BDC Definition
===========================================================
1. Open browser to SharePoint Central Admininstration site
2. Browse to your Shared Services Administration site
3. Under Business Data Catalog, click on Import Application Data
4. Browse to the ProductCatalogDefinition.xml file.
5. Leave the "File Type" and "Resources to import" options with default values
6. Click "Import".
